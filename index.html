THE PHOTOGRAPHER

Behind the Lens: rizaler_

I am a visual storyteller based in Pekanbaru, specializing in documentary street photography and fine art architectural framing. With over a decade of capturing the world's quiet dramas, my work explores the interplay between shadow, structure, and human essence.

Every frame is an intentional dialogue with light. By focusing on minimal, bold compositions and striking contrasts, I strive to reveal the extraordinary narratives hiding in our everyday environments.
